Meet the Owners

John and Jane met on a mission to Mars. John, a former sheep shearer, went on the trip to get some clarity about his next adventures in life. Jane — a Peloton instructor — went on the trip to explore new landscape.

While on the trip, John and Jane realized that they had one common love — the love of Earth and the love of being in Space.

Returning back to their home state of Oklahoma, John and Jane hatched a plan to launch a luxury liner centered around their passions.

In 2030, John and Jane Smith officially launched INFINITY VOYAGER, the lustful cruise line for outdoor exploration around the cosmos.

To this day, John and Jane return to Mars yearly to ensure that they never forget the roots of their love and where they came from.
